Chain of Lakes

Masts, 2024

Compressed charcoal on newsprint. 18 x 24 inches.

I began sailing on the "Chain of Lakes" in Dane County, Wisconsin, in the summer of 2023. Sailing in the evening and into the night is a regular occurence. As myself and my fellow sailors float on the water, we witness the lake and sky become dark together.

This is an ongoing series.
